<p><p>The industrial-grade magnesium chloride market can be segmented into two primary types: anhydrous magnesium chloride and hexahydrate magnesium chloride. Anhydrous magnesium chloride is a solid, dry form commonly used in various applications, including dust control, as a de-icing agent, and in manufacturing processes. Hexahydrate magnesium chloride, on the other hand, contains water molecules and is typically utilized in applications requiring higher solubility, such as in brine production and chemical synthesis. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on properties and requirements.</p></p>
