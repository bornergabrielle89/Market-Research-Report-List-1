<p><p>The firefighter helmets market features two primary types: structural firefighting helmets and jet-style firefighting helmets. Structural firefighting helmets are designed for protection during structural fires, offering enhanced durability, heat resistance, and visor options. In contrast, jet-style firefighting helmets are lighter and feature a streamlined design, primarily used in wildland firefighting and rescue operations. Both helmet types focus on safety and functionality, catering to different firefighting scenarios while ensuring optimal head protection and visibility for firefighters in the field.</p></p>
