<p><p>Electric curtain tracks come in three main market types based on their opening mechanisms: left opening, right opening, and middle opening. Left opening tracks draw curtains from the left side, ideal for spaces where left-side access is preferred. Right opening tracks function similarly, but curtains open from the right, catering to opposite room configurations. Middle opening tracks draw curtains apart from the center, offering balanced functionality and aesthetic appeal, making them suitable for various window sizes and design preferences.</p></p>
