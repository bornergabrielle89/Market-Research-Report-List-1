<p><ul><li>Petroleum-based Materials</li><li>PHA Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biodegradable stakes market features various material types, primarily categorized into petroleum-based materials and PHA plastics. Petroleum-based materials are derived from fossil fuels and can break down under specific conditions, offering a budget-friendly option for temporary uses. On the other hand, PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoates) plastics are bio-based and produced by microbial fermentation, providing a more environmentally friendly alternative. PHA stakes decompose naturally in a composting environment, appealing to eco-conscious consumers and industries focused on sustainability.</p></p>
