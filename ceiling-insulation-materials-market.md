<p><p>The ceiling insulation materials market includes various types such as EPS/XPS, RockWool, PIR/PU, GlassWool, and others. EPS/XPS (Expanded/Extruded Polystyrene) are lightweight, moisture-resistant, and cost-effective. RockWool, made from volcanic rock, offers excellent fire resistance and soundproofing. PIR/PU (Polyisocyanurate/Polyurethane) provides high thermal efficiency and is ideal for energy-saving applications. GlassWool, made from recycled glass, is affordable, fire-resistant, and offers good thermal and acoustic insulation. Other materials include natural fibers and aerogels, catering to niche applications with specific performance requirements. Each type serves distinct needs based on thermal, acoustic, and fire safety properties.</p></p>
