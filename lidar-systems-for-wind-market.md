<p><p>Lidar systems for wind market are crucial for assessing wind resources and optimizing turbine placement. Ground-mounted Lidar systems are installed at various locations on land to measure wind profiles over a wide area, providing valuable data for site evaluation and energy production forecasting. In contrast, nacelle-mounted Lidar systems are integrated directly onto wind turbine nacelles.
