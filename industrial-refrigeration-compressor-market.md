<p><p>The industrial refrigeration compressor market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Rotary compressors feature rotating mechanisms, providing compact design and quieter operation, ideal for small to medium applications. Screw compressors employ twin screws, delivering consistent performance and high capacity, making them suitable for large-scale refrigeration.
