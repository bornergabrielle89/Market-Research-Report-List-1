<p><p>The soluble rosin flux market is categorized into three main types: liquid, paste, and solid. Liquid flux is easy to apply and ideal for automated processes, providing quick soldering solutions. Paste flux, often used in manual soldering, offers better control and adhesion, making it suitable for intricate assemblies. Solid flux, typically in stick form, is used for specific applications requiring heat to activate the rosin. Each type caters to different soldering needs and application methods, enhancing overall soldering performance.</p></p>
