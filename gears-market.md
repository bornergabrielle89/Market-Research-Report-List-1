<p><strong>The Gears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spur Gear</li><li>Helical Gear</li><li>Bevel Gear</li><li>Worm Gear</li><li>Gear Rack</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gears market includes various types designed for specific applications. **Spur gears** are simple, cost-effective, and used for low-speed, high-torque systems. **Helical gears** offer smoother, quieter operation due to angled teeth, ideal for high-speed applications. **Bevel gears** transmit motion between intersecting shafts, commonly in differential drives. **Worm gears** provide high reduction ratios and are used in compact, high-torque systems. **Gear racks** convert rotational motion into linear motion, used in steering systems. **Others** include specialized gears like planetary or hypoid, catering to niche industrial needs.</p></p>
