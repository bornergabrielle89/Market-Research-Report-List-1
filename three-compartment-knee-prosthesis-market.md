<p><ul><li>Cemented Bone</li><li>Non-Cemented Bone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The three-compartment knee prosthesis market consists of cemented and non-cemented bone types. Cemented prostheses utilize bone cement to anchor the implant securely, providing immediate stability and is favored in older patients with compromised bone quality. Non-cemented prostheses rely on osseointegration, allowing natural bone to grow into the implant surface, promoting long-term stability and being suitable for younger, more active patients. Both types are vital in addressing knee joint degeneration, enhancing mobility, and improving patients' quality of life.</p></p>
