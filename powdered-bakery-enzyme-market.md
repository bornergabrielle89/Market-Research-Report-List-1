<p><p>The powdered bakery enzyme market includes three primary types: bakery protease enzymes, bakery carbohydrase enzymes, and bakery lipase enzymes. Bakery protease enzymes enhance dough elasticity and improve texture, while bakery carbohydrase enzymes, such as amylases, aid in starch breakdown, promoting fermentation and extending shelf life. Bakery lipase enzymes play a crucial role in fat breakdown, influencing flavor and dough stability. Together, these enzymes optimize baking processes, improve product quality, and enhance the overall sensory attributes of baked goods.</p></p>
