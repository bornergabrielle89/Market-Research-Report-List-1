<p><ul><li>Capacitive Force Sensors</li><li>Piezo-Resistive Force Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive force sensor market includes various types like capacitive force sensors, piezo-resistive force sensors, and others. Capacitive force sensors detect changes in capacitance when force is applied, making them ideal for precise measurements. Piezo-resistive force sensors utilize changes in electrical resistance under stress and are commonly used for dynamic force measurements. Other types may include strain gauge sensors or optical sensors, each offering unique advantages for applications in vehicle safety systems, diagnostics, and performance monitoring.</p></p>
