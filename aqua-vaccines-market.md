<p><p>The aqua vaccines market consists of three main types: injectable vaccines, dip vaccines, and oral vaccines. Injectable vaccines are administered directly into fish or shrimp, providing targeted protection against diseases. Dip vaccines involve immersing aquatic animals in a medicinal solution, promoting immunity through skin absorption. Oral vaccines are delivered in feed, allowing aquatic organisms to gain immunity as they consume the medicated food. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of convenience, effectiveness, and application in aquaculture health management.</p></p>
