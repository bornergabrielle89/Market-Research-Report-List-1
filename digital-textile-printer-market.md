<p><p>The digital textile printer market consists of various printing techniques, with sublimation inkjet printing and direct-to-garment (DTG) printing being prominent. Sublimation inkjet printing utilizes heat to transfer dye onto synthetic fabrics, producing vibrant, long-lasting designs suitable for sportswear and fashion. In contrast, DTG printing applies ink directly onto garments using specialized inkjet technology, allowing for intricate designs and color variations on cotton fabrics. Both methods cater to diverse applications, from custom apparel to large-scale textile production.</p></p>
