<p><p>The polyolefin hot melt adhesives market comprises three main types: granular, blocky, and film. Granular adhesives are typically used for easy handling and precise application, ideal for automated processes. Blocky adhesives offer convenient packaging and can be melted in bulk for larger applications, providing strong bonds in various industries. Film adhesives are designed for thin, uniform layers and are suitable for applications requiring flexibility, such as laminating and packaging, ensuring effective adhesion while maintaining product integrity.</p></p>
