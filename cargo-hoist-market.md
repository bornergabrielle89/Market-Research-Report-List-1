<p><p>The cargo hoist market is primarily categorized into horizontal and vertical types, based on the orientation of their operation. Horizontal cargo hoists are designed to transport goods across a flat plane, making them ideal for warehouses and loading docks where horizontal movement is required. In contrast, vertical cargo hoists lift materials straight up and down, commonly used in multi-story buildings for efficient delivery between floors. Both types facilitate the movement of goods, enhancing operational efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
