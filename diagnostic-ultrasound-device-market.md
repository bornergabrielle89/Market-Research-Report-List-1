<p><p>The diagnostic ultrasound device market is primarily segmented into black and white (B/W) and color ultrasound systems. Black and white ultrasound, also known as grayscale imaging, provides high-resolution images primarily used for anatomical assessment and guiding procedures. In contrast, color ultrasound utilizes Doppler technology to visualize blood flow and highlight vascular structures, enabling dynamic assessments of physiological conditions. Both types are crucial in clinical settings, offering distinct advantages for various diagnostic applications in obstetrics, cardiology, and beyond.</p></p>
