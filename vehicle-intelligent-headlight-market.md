<p><p>The Vehicle Intelligent Headlight market includes two key types: Adaptive Front-lighting Systems (AFS) and Adaptive Driving Beam Systems (ADBS). AFS enhances nighttime visibility by adjusting the headlight angle based on vehicle speed and steering input, illuminating curves and turns. In contrast, ADBS optimizes light distribution by dynamically changing the beam pattern to avoid blinding oncoming drivers while maintaining visibility of road signs and pedestrians. Both technologies improve safety and driving comfort by providing tailored illumination solutions.</p></p>
