<p><p>The Diesel Vehicle Exhaust Gas Aftertreatment System market includes technologies designed to reduce harmful emissions. **Particulate Oxidation Catalyst (POC)** oxidizes particulate matter (PM) and hydrocarbons. **Lean NOx Trap (LNT)** captures and reduces nitrogen oxides (NOx) during lean-burn conditions. **Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)** uses a urea-based solution to convert NOx into nitrogen and water. **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF)** traps and burns PM. **Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)** reduces CO and hydrocarbons. **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR)** lowers NOx by recirculating exhaust gas into the combustion chamber. These systems collectively enhance emission control and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
