<p><p>Analytical HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) systems are essential for separating and analyzing compounds in various industries. The market is primarily divided into two types: Normal Phase HPLC and Reverse Phase HPLC. Normal Phase HPLC uses a polar stationary phase and a non-polar mobile phase, suitable for separating polar compounds. In contrast, Reverse Phase HPLC employs a non-polar stationary phase and a polar mobile phase, making it ideal for separating non-polar compounds and widely used in pharmaceuticals, biochemistry, and environmental testing.</p></p>
