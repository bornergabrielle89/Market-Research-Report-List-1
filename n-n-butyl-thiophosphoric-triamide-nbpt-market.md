<p><p>N-(n-butyl) Thiophosphoric Triamide (NBPT) is a chemical compound primarily used as a urease inhibitor in agricultural practices, particularly in fertilizers. This efficiency not only promotes sustainable farming but also improves crop yields, making it a valuable additive in the agricultural sector.</p><p>The demand for NBPT has been steadily increasing, driven by the rising need for efficient fertilizers amidst growing global food production challenges.
