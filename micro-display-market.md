<p><p>The micro-display market consists primarily of reflective and transmissive display types. Reflective displays utilize ambient light for better visibility, making them energy-efficient and ideal for outdoor applications, as they rely on light reflecting off the display surface. In contrast, transmissive displays work by allowing light to pass through the display, creating vibrant colors and higher brightness levels, suitable for indoor use. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various industries including augmented reality, wearables, and portable devices.</p></p>
