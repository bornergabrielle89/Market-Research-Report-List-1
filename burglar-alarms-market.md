<p><p>The burglar alarms market comprises various types of detection devices. Hermetically sealed reed switches provide reliable security by responding to magnetic fields. Microwave detectors emit microwave pulses to identify intruders, and compact surveillance radar offers heightened monitoring capabilities. Photo-electric beams create invisible barriers that trigger alarms upon interruption. Together, these technologies enhance security measures in residential, commercial, and industrial settings, ensuring comprehensive protection.</p></p>
