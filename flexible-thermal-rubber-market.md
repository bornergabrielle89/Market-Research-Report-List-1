<p><p>The Flexible Thermal Rubber market is categorized into several types based on the polymer composition, including NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber), EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), Chloroprene, and Others. NBR-based rubber is known for its oil resistance, making it suitable for automotive applications. EPDM is valued for its weather resistance and flexibility, ideal for roofing and seals. Chloroprene offers good chemical stability and is commonly used in adhesives. The "Others" category encompasses various specialty rubbers catering to niche applications.</p></p>
