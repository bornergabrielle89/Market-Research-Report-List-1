<p><p>The Carbon Nanotube (CNT) Array market is primarily divided into single-walled and multi-walled types. Single-walled carbon nanotubes (SWCNTs) consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylinder, offering unique electronic properties, ideal for applications in nanoelectronics and sensors. Multi-walled carbon nanotubes (MWCNTs) comprise several concentric cylinders, providing greater mechanical strength and conductivity, making them suitable for composite materials and energy storage systems. Each type serves distinct applications, influencing their market dynamics and growth potential.</p></p>
