<p><p>The microcentrifuge tubes market comprises three main types based on their base design: conical base, round bottom base, and flat bottom base. Conical base tubes facilitate efficient sedimentation by allowing samples to be spun to the tip, making them ideal for collecting pellets. Round bottom base tubes are versatile and suitable for various applications, offering good sample recovery. Flat bottom base tubes provide stable support for reactions but may not be as effective for sedimentation, making them suitable for specific uses.</p></p>
