<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Truck & Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel rotation, providing precise speed information, and are often favored for their accuracy and reliability in various conditions. Magnetic Electric sensors, on the other hand, rely on changes in magnetic fields generated by the moving wheel, offering a cost-effective solution. Both technologies are essential for effective vehicle control and safety features in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
