<p><p>The corrective contact lens market comprises three main types: rigid contact lenses, soft contact lenses, and hybrid contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses offer precise vision correction and are durable, making them suitable for conditions like astigmatism. Soft contact lenses are widely popular due to their comfort and flexibility, ideal for daily wear. Hybrid contact lenses combine the benefits of both, featuring a rigid center for sharp vision and a soft outer ring for comfort, catering to diverse vision needs.</p></p>
