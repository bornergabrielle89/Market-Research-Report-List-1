<p><p>Rubber-based pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) are categorized into natural and synthetic types. Natural rubber-based PSAs offer excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for various applications, such as tapes and labels. They are environmentally friendly but can have temperature limitations. Synthetic rubber-based PSAs, derived from polymers like styrene-butadiene or acrylics, provide enhanced performance, greater durability, and resistance to heat and aging, making them ideal for industrial applications and demanding environments. Both types play crucial roles across various industries.</p></p>
