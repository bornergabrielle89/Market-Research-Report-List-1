<p><p>The Multilayer Ceramic Capacitor (MLCC) market is divided into two primary types: Class 1 and Class 2. Class 1 capacitors offer stable capacitance with minimal temperature and voltage dependency, making them ideal for precision applications. In contrast, Class 2 capacitors provide higher capacitance values and are more cost-effective, though they exhibit greater variability in capacitance with temperature and voltage changes. Each class serves different applications in electronics, influencing their demand and market growth trajectories based on performance needs.</p></p>
