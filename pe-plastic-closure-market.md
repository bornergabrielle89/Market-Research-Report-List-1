<p><p>The PE plastic closure market encompasses different molding techniques, primarily compression molding and injection molding. Compression molding involves placing plastic material into a heated mold, where it is shaped and solidified under pressure, ideal for producing sturdier closures with consistent quality. In contrast, injection molding injects molten plastic into a mold, allowing for complex designs and faster production rates. Both methods cater to diverse applications, including beverage, food, and pharmaceutical packaging, driven by functionality, cost-effectiveness, and consumer preferences.</p></p>
