<p><ul><li>Single Acting</li><li>Double Acting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrically operated diaphragm pumps are classified into two main types: single acting and double acting. Single acting diaphragm pumps use one diaphragm that creates a pumping action in one stroke, allowing for a simple and compact design, ideal for low flow applications. In contrast, double acting diaphragm pumps employ two diaphragms working alternately, enabling continuous flow and higher efficiency, making them suitable for demanding industrial processes. Both types benefit from reliability and versatility in handling various fluids.</p></p>
