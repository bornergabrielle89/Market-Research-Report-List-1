<p><ul><li>Mass Flow Dependent Detectors</li><li>Concentration Dependent Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas chromatography detector market is categorized into mass flow dependent detectors and concentration dependent detectors. Mass flow dependent detectors measure the quantity of gas flowing through the system, providing real-time analysis of flow rates and concentrations. In contrast, concentration dependent detectors focus on the concentration of specific analytes in a given sample, offering greater sensitivity and specificity. Both types serve essential roles in various applications, including environmental monitoring, food safety, and pharmaceuticals, enhancing the accuracy of analytical processes.</p></p>
