<p><p>Dormant alfalfa seeds are seeds of the alfalfa plant that have been bred to exhibit dormancy, a trait that delays germination until favorable environmental conditions are met. Dormant alfalfa seeds are widely used in agriculture for forage production, soil improvement, and as a high-protein feed for livestock.</p><p>The Dormant Alfalfa Seed Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for high-quality forage crops, rising livestock production, and the need for sustainable agricultural practices.
