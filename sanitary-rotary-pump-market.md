<p><p>The sanitary rotary pump market is categorized into two main types based on the sealing mechanism: single mechanical seals and double mechanical seals. Single mechanical seals provide a simpler design and are efficient for low-pressure applications, commonly used in food and beverage processing. In contrast, double mechanical seals offer enhanced leak protection and are ideal for high-pressure environments, ensuring greater hygiene and safety in sensitive applications like pharmaceuticals. Both types are essential to maintain product integrity and meet industry standards.</p></p>
