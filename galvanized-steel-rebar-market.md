<p><strong>The Galvanized Steel Rebar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li><li>Continuous Hot-dip Galvanized Rebar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized steel rebar is coated with zinc to enhance corrosion resistance, extending its lifespan in harsh environments. Conventional hot-dip galvanized rebar involves immersing the steel in molten zinc, creating a thick, durable coating. Continuous hot-dip galvanized rebar undergoes a similar process but is produced in a continuous line, ensuring uniform coating and higher efficiency. Both types are widely used in construction, with conventional methods offering robust protection and continuous methods providing cost-effective, large-scale production for infrastructure projects.</p></p>
