<p><p>UVC air and surface disinfection systems are categorized into three main types: ceiling-mounted, wall-mounted, and stand-up configurations. Ceiling-mounted systems are installed directly above the area requiring disinfection, effectively covering large spaces without obstructing floor-level movement. Wall-mounted systems are affixed to vertical surfaces, targeting specific areas while saving floor space. Stand-up units are portable and can be relocated as needed, providing flexibility for disinfecting various locations. Each type employs UVC light technology to eliminate pathogens, enhancing overall hygiene and safety.</p></p>
